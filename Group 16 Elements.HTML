Group 16 Elements - The p-Block Elements - Chemistry Class 12 Quiz

1. The elements belonging to Group 16 of the periodic table are collectively known as the __________ family or chalcogens.
oxygen
2. The general electronic configuration of Group 16 elements is $ns^2 np^?$.
4
3. The first element of Group 16 is __________.
oxygen (O)
4. The tendency to show catenation __________ down the group in Group 16.
decreases
5. Oxygen exists as a diatomic molecule, $O_2$, which is __________ in nature.
paramagnetic
6. Ozone ($O_3$) is an allotrope of __________.
oxygen
7. Ozone layer in the stratosphere protects us from harmful __________ radiation.
ultraviolet (UV)
8. Sulfur exists as $S_8$ molecules due to its tendency to form __________ bonds.
single
9. Rhombic and monoclinic are two common __________ of sulfur.
allotropes
10. Sulfur dioxide ($SO_2$) is produced by burning sulfur in __________.
air or oxygen
11. Sulfur dioxide is used in the manufacture of __________ acid.
sulfuric ($H_2SO_4$)
12. Sulfuric acid is known as the __________ of chemicals.
king
13. Sulfuric acid is manufactured industrially by the __________ process.
Contact
14. In the Contact process, the catalyst used for the oxidation of $SO_2$ to $SO_3$ is __________.
vanadium(V) oxide ($V_2O_5$)
15. Sulfuric acid is a strong __________ agent due to its high affinity for water.
dehydrating
16. The oxoacids of sulfur include sulfurous acid ($H_2SO_3$) and sulfuric acid ($H_2SO_4$). Sulfurous acid has sulfur in the __________ oxidation state.
+4
17. The oxoacids of sulfur include sulfurous acid ($H_2SO_3$) and sulfuric acid ($H_2SO_4$). Sulfuric acid has sulfur in the __________ oxidation state.
+6
18. The hybridization of oxygen in water ($H_2O$) is __________.
$sp^3$
19. The bond angle in water ($H_2O$) is less than the tetrahedral angle due to the repulsion between the two lone pairs and the two __________ pairs of electrons.
bond
20. The hydride of oxygen, water ($H_2O$), is a liquid at room temperature due to strong __________ bonding.
hydrogen
21. The thermal stability of the hydrides of Group 16 elements __________ down the group.
decreases
22. The acidic character of the hydrides of Group 16 elements __________ down the group.
increases
23. Sulfur forms a number of halides. The most stable dichloride is $SCl_2$, while the corresponding oxygen compound is __________.
$OCl_2$ (unstable)
24. Sulfur hexafluoride ($SF_6$) is an exceptionally stable compound due to __________ reasons.
steric and kinetic
25. Selenium and tellurium also form various compounds similar to sulfur, but their metallic character __________ down the group.
increases
