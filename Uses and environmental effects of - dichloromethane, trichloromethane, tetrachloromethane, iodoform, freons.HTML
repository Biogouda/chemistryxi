Uses and Environmental Effects of Polyhalogen Compounds - Chemistry Class 12 Quiz

Dichloromethane ($CH_2Cl_2$)

1. Dichloromethane is used as a solvent in the __________ industry.
pharmaceutical
2. Dichloromethane is used in the food industry for the decaffeination of __________ and tea.
coffee
3. Inhalation of high concentrations of dichloromethane can affect the __________ nervous system.
central
4. Dichloromethane is considered a greenhouse gas and contributes to __________.
global warming

Trichloromethane ($CHCl_3$)

5. Trichloromethane, also known as __________, was formerly used as an anesthetic.
chloroform
6. Today, trichloromethane is primarily used in the industrial production of other __________.
chemicals
7. Acute exposure to trichloromethane can cause dysfunction of the __________.
liver
8. Trichloromethane in water can have moderate toxicity to __________ life.
aquatic

Tetrachloromethane ($CCl_4$)

9. Tetrachloromethane, also known as carbon tetrachloride, was historically used in __________ extinguishers.
fire
10. Tetrachloromethane was a precursor to __________, which are now largely phased out.
chlorofluorocarbons (CFCs)
11. Exposure to high concentrations of tetrachloromethane can cause significant damage to the liver and __________.
kidneys
12. Tetrachloromethane is harmful to the __________ layer.
ozone

Iodoform ($CHI_3$)

13. Iodoform was historically used as an __________ due to the liberation of free iodine.
antiseptic
14. Iodoform has a characteristic __________ odor.
unpleasant (sweetish, like chloroform)
15. High exposure to iodoform can affect the __________ nervous system.
central

Freons (Chlorofluorocarbons - CFCs)

16. Freons were widely used as __________ in refrigerators and air conditioners.
refrigerants
17. Freons were also used as propellants in __________.
aerosols
18. Freons are major contributors to the depletion of the __________ layer in the stratosphere.
ozone
19. The production of CFCs has been largely __________ in many countries due to their environmental impact.
banned

DDT (Dichlorodiphenyltrichloroethane)

20. DDT is a persistent organic __________ that was widely used as an insecticide.
pollutant
21. DDT is known to __________ in the fatty tissues of animals.
bioaccumulate
22. DDT has been linked to harmful effects on wildlife, particularly __________ birds.
predatory
23. The use of DDT has been __________ in many countries due to its environmental persistence and toxicity.
banned or severely restricted

BHC (Benzene Hexachloride)

24. BHC is another organochlorine compound that has been used as an __________.
insecticide
25. Similar to DDT, BHC is a persistent organic pollutant and can __________ in the environment.
bioaccumulate
